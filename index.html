Learn to Hit Your Irons Like a Pro

We have all been there... you're out on the course. You have a beautiful drive and now you are setting up your approach shot. Whack... you top your iron. Shank... you hit the ball 50 yards to your right. Smack... you sliced your shot into the parking lot. Well those days are over. We are going to teach you the basic steps to help you hit your irons better, and lower that number on the scorecard

Things to Keep in Mind

  • Always keep your head down
  • Follow through the ball with a clean path
  • Limit the movement of your lower body
